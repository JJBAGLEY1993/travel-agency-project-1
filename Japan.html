Japan

Japan is an island country in East asia. Located in the pacific ocean
off the east coast of the Asian mainland, it is bordered
to the west of Japan and extends from the sea of Okhotsk in the north to the East of China sea in the south

This beautiful country is made up of 6,800 islands. Japan itself is a country
for all walks of life. If you like hiking and mountain climbing, you
could visit mount fuji. For those who like history of different cultures
may want to visit the imperial palace in tokyo.
